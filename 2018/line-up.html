    Frequently Answered Questions

    • Are the pre-parties part of the Pass?
      No, the pre-parties are a part of no formula of pass, you have to pay separatedly at the entrance of every party (on Tuesday and Wednesday 12 €, on Thursday 15 €)
    • To what gives the right to me the Pass Bootcamp?
      The pass Bootcamp gives the right to you in all the activities of the days of the Congress, (workshops, shows and parties).
    • May I got back my pass "bracelet" before Friday?
      Yes you can already get back your bracelet during preparties on Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday evening.
