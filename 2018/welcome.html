PISC 2018

To the Stars

Welcome to the 3rd edition of the Paris International Salsa Congress.

The long-awaited event of the year with more than 2000 enthusiasts coming from all around the world, will take place April 17th to the 22nd, 2018.

Six days of madness and delight await you from beginning to end, with our famous pre-parties taking place in the best venues in the capital. Shows and excellent music is guaranteed to delight you!

Friday morning, we will be in the Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile, where the decorations and beauty of this 4-star hotel, and our main venue, will amaze you from the beginning to give you a full sensation of uniqueness and magic.

You will take advantage of a large variety of workshops offered by our international artists during the days, and in the evenings, you will be entertained by an amazing line-up of shows, with parties until 7 am. We have four rooms with different atmospheres and styles (Mambo, Cuban, Bachata, Kizomba).

 An unforgettable experience not to be missed. Welcome to the PISC.

    Frequently Answered Questions

    • Are the pre-parties part of the Pass?
      No, the pre-parties are a part of no formula of pass, you have to pay separatedly at the entrance of every party (on Tuesday and Wednesday 12 €, on Thursday 15 €)
    • To what gives the right to me the Pass Bootcamp?
      The pass Bootcamp gives the right to you in all the activities of the days of the Congress, (workshops, shows and parties).
    • May I got back my pass "bracelet" before Friday?
      Yes you can already get back your bracelet during preparties on Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday evening.
