  • FAQ

    Are the pre-parties a part of the pass?

    No, the pre-parties are not included in any pass, you have to pay separately at the entrance of every evening (12 € on Tuesday and Wednesday and 15 € on Thursday).

    To what do I have access with the Bootcamp pass?

    The Bootcamp pass gives you access to all workshops, shows and parties on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

    Can I pick up my bracelet pass before Friday?

    Yes, you can already get your bracelet during the pre-parties on Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday evenings.
