Remember April, 2016... It was the first edition of PISC, Paris International Salsa Congress, that took place at the Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile, in the heart of the city of light! If you were not with us...

It was a whole week of sheer madness, with more than 2000 people that came from all over the world, sharing the same passion. Beautiful people, with smiles on their faces and dancing non-stop to the intoxicating rhythms of our incredible DJs.

For the second edition, we propose you a timeless week. Everything begins with 3 pre-parties to kickoff the congress, to start you off in some of the best places selected in the capital. Then in the day you will have all the time to accompany your foreign friends to see the sights of Paris.

On the weekend we take everything to a whole new level! You can take advantage of a wide variety of workshops taught by our international artists, then in the evening, when the congress is going full swing, come and be amazed by the fabulous shows and take advantage of the full night to the very end of our evenings with a variety of rhythms and musical offerings (Salsa, Bachata, Kizomba). An unforgettable experience not to be missed! Come join us to live this magic moment during the second edition of PISC on 18 to April 23rd, 2017.

I look forward to seeing you there.

 

Mouaze Konat
PISC Organizer & Producer
