FRANAIS

Badget

In my humble opinion, Paris is the city where meet harmoniously and in a the most natural way, the history, the art, the culture and naturally the dance. Whoever visited Paris knows that this city does not stop being a surprise by its unique beauty and its charm.

PISC (Paris International Salsa Congress) is the major Congress of Salsa in Paris, where the French and international salseros can share their common passion. The dance Bachata and Kizomba is also for the honor because they are unmistakably a member of our universe.

It is now the second edition of PISC! My close friend and co-organizer Yoshi and i are happy and proud that this project was already successful at its first edition.

Naturally, this project would not have been able to amount alone. I am extremely fortunate to have by my side a talented and reliable team, putting their heart and their passion to help me in this adventure.

Being myself an artist in the latin world for more than 15 years, have the privilege to travel in numerous countries to pass on my passion and create unique links with fabulous people.

Some of you know maybe ' my previous life ', when I practised extensively the martial art "karate", the values of which are: the humility, the respect, the sincerity and the courage.

I wish to undertake to each of you, my team and me, motivated by these values to do everything in our power so that PISC is and stays year after year, an inescapable event of happiness, quality and delight.

Well on, this congress would be impossible without your presence, that is why we need your support and your joy of life.

Mouaze Konat

Salsa/Chachacha dancer, teacher and choreographer. Organizer of the Paris International Salsa Congress

Organizer Team

Creative Team

Mouaze Konat

Mouaze is a PISC Organizer and producer himself. His status of international artist taking him to be regularly invited in the biggest congress of part the world he one to give through of long years, the experience and the necessary vision to start and organiz is home Event.

Demanding, perfectionist and determined, he gives the guideline to follow to make the PISC a unmissable event. With him energy and passion, Mouaze guides all the organizing team in this beautiful human adventure and this big ambition project.

ELEONORA JAUK

Dancer of Latin ballroom in her beginning, Ella is today, since more than 4 years partner of Mouaze Konat and sharing with him the most beautiful stage of Salsa Congress in the world.

Her exigence is reflected in her manager's post of the planning of workshops and shows of the artists, as well as the program of the DJs.

She organizes various price lists and passes, omnipresent operator of the PISC.

Ella also watches that there is no forgetting in the preparation of this event which is going to gather more than 2000 people.

Christophe Chikli

Founder of My-Acade-My (platform of reservation and management of dancing lessons) and consulting quality in the automobile, Christophe puts in the service of the congress of Paris his qualities of management at the post of leader of the organizing team of the PISC.

It is with flexibility, listens to and benevolence with the other members of the team that he naturally won their respects and their attentions.

Muriel Dim

French with an Italian flavor, Muriel loves to be on stage!

She began learning salsa at the end of her studies and chose to perfect her technique at the Mouaze School, under Mouaze and Ella's guidance, where she rapidly becomes part of the Mouaze Dancers dance team in 2013.

Muriel puts her skills in human resources management at the service of the PISC.

She is in charge of managing our team of helpful volunteers who will do everything to make this congress an unforgettable experience for you.

Elonore Morales

Born in the Balearic islands, Lo grew up in Paris and took classical and modern jazz lessons before discovering the salsa world.

In 2009, she falls in love with the NY Style, first with Sve, then with Mouaze who will become her mentor.

In Parallel, she recently started to learn the lift and tricks technique linked to accro yoga practice and also developed a liking for the samba rhythms.

Expert in the organisation of tailor made trips, Lo is in charge of the artists from arrival to departure. She is our Artist relationship coordinator.or.

Stphane Cou

Native from the west indies, Stphane is a dance addict since 2003. Cuban salsa, tango and a mambo enthusiast. He is one of the soldier of the Mouaze School where he keeps learning and developing his technique ("Again and Again"). In parallel, he discovered Afro Cuban, which makes him think that he still has a long way to go before mastering all these different rhythms.

Specialised in the new information and communication technologies, Stphane put his IT skills at the service of the PISC. He works for the Web and Digital department.

Yegor Kozlov

Having decided to leave his career of bar manager to continue his dream and dedicate all of his time to dance, travels and congresses, Yegor was attracted to the PISC in 2016 as this project corresponds completely to his passion of dance and division.

Yegor is responsible for the coordination of the team of the volunteers during the weekend and puts his working experience in contribution to offer you a service and a choice of the consumptions of great distinction.

 

Julien Sarton

Julien has been dancing Hip Hop and salsa for 12 years now. He trained himself at the Mouaze School for 3 years before leaving for new adventures in Argentina where he got inspired by new influences, among which Rumba and Afro Cuban.

Back in Paris since 2014, Julien puts his entrepreneurial spirit (he is the founder of Fit Factory, a sport marketing agency) and his talent as event organizer at the service of the PISC.

David Dijoux

Native of Martinique, David discovered the lesson of salsa in 2003 in Toulouse thanks to her youngest sister. He began with Cuban salsa then mambo.

Since it is a relentless of the festivals salsa mambo in all Europe. David is regularly involved as volunteer (often team leader) in many festivals.

Besides, he is a president of an association of Toulouse which organizes evenings mambo monthly and was also in 2015 a manager of a team of amateur dancers.

It is thus strong of the acquired experience and further to the recommendation of his friend (Stphane Cou) that David joined the team of organization of the PISC from the 1st edition to bring his help to this adventure.

Pablo Montero

art direction / web designer

Lover of the visual art, Pablo is a fine observer of the world of the design and more particularly the beauty of the movement.

In our first edition, the PISC website gives tribute to Paris and the dance, through the artistic genius of the French painter Toulouse Lautrec.

For our second edition it is on the theme "The Life in Pink" that the design of our website is managed.

So every year, our website offers you an unique visual experience which you shall rediscover during the festivities of the PISC.

Nera Kraljevic

Logo Designer

Fascinated by the movement, but also by the lights and non verbal communication, Nera pursued a career in the visual arts.

Having a Master's degree in the graphics and the photography, she is an enthusiast who formidably put her talents as a contribution to create for us the famous logo, identity of the PISC.
