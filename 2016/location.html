FRANÇAIS

Venue

PISC 2016 will welcome you at the majestic Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile, a 4 stars luxury hotel, formerly renowned as Hotel Concorde Lafayette!

Benefiting from a direct access by shuttle bus from Charles de Gaulle Airport, this is without any doubt one of the most prestigious location you can find in the very heart of Paris, just minutes from both Arc de Triomphe and the world-famous Champs-Élysées!

Connected to the Palais des Congres, one of the largest entertainment venue in Paris, this unique location will allow you to both enjoy and discover easily the capital's best spots in between dancing!

Lastly and most importantly, the amazing dance floor..! Mesdames et Messieurs, we will prepare for you nearly 3,000 square meters of Dancing floor divided in 3 atmospheres:Salsa Mambo, Salsa Cubana, Kizomba & Bachata! So don’t forget your extra pairs of dancing shoes!

 

HYATT REGENCY PARIS ETOILES ★★★★

Isn’t this the coolest thing to be just one elevator away from your nice and comfortable room to the salsa congress craziness happening “downstairs”?

Imagine for instance that you can go back to your room at your ease to take a nice and refreshing shower between workshops or in between “muy caliente” dances whenever you feel like.. or whatever reasons! This is what we call luxury my friend.. :)

Our partner Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile knows it as well, that is why they are kindly offering to all salsa and kizomba dancers exceptionally low and competitive prices, so a maximum of you can enjoy this great opportunity!

From the Airport / By Taxi

From Roissy-Charles de Gaulle airport (CDG) a taxi journey takes approximately 40 minutes, the cost should be in the region of €50.00 to €55.00, depending on traffic.

Orly airport (ORY) is approximately 40 minutes by taxi and fares should be close to €35.00, depending on traffic.

"Les Cars Air France" / Air France bus

Buses operated by Air France are also available and the stop is right on the side of the hotel (Line N 2 - Stop Porte Maillot),  and go direct to or from Roissy-Charles de Gaulle airport.
Approximate journey time is 45 minutes.
Fare: 17 Euros.

Please book in advance at:
http://en.lescarsairfrance.com/

*Please note that buses leaving from Orly Airport won’t stop nearby the hotel but directly at Champs-Elysees.

By Metro

Line 1 (La Défense / Chateau de Vincennes) - Porte Maillot station

Subway RER - Line C - Porte Maillot Station

Porte Maillot metro station is a five-minute walk away and can be accessed via undercover walkway directly from the hotel.

Car Parking

1000 square metres of indoor parking space adjacent to the hotel and under cover charge (daily fee: €50). Doormen and luggage porters are on hand 24 hours a day to assist guests with their luggage when they arrive and depart.
