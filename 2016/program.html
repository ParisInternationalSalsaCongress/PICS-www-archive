AGUA

@ BOAT CONCORDE ATLANTIQUE

Let’s dance on the Seine River! The “Peniche Concorde Atlantique”, located walking distance from the famous Tuilerie Garden, Orsay Museum or bridge Alexander III,  is the boat where all Salsa and kizomba lovers meet every Tuesday.

It has two main dance floors for Salsa and Kizomba with a lovely terrace on top to get acquainted with your new friends around a nice drink. Let’s play a remake all together of the Salsa version of “Love Boat” led by their captains, Leon & Noémie. AGUA!!!

WELCOME TO PARIS

@ HYATT REGENGY PARIS ETOILE

Here we are, the first official day of Paris International Salsa Congress 2016!

To all the dancers coming from many different regions of France and to all the ones coming from abroad (some coming from the other side of the planet) we shout to them big and loud: Bienvenue a Paris! Welcome to Paris!

A full day of festivities is waiting for you with 2 boot camps, many great workshops so you don’t want to miss any of them and of course the amazing performance shows from your beloved artists.

DRESS CODE: BLEU, BLANC, ROUGE

Then we invite everyone to come and dress up for the night party that everyone is waiting for, inspiring by the colors of the French Flag: Sensual Blue, Virgin White, Passion Red.. or a subtle mix of those 3.. we let your imagination surprise us!

09:00 - 14:30 / 20:00 - 3:00 > Registration

10:30 - 12:40 > Bootcamp Karel Flores

13:40 - 19:15 > Workshops

13:40 - 17:00 > Bootcamp Alma Latina

17:10 - 19:10 > Bootcamp Karel Flores

22:00 - 7:00 > Welcome to Paris Party

23:00 - 00:30 > Showtime

The tickets will be available at the venue door. Around 40 €. During the shows one room will be open for dance with mix music.

GLAMOUR & CHIC

@ HYATT REGENGY PARIS ETOILE

The second day of Paris International Salsa Congress 2016 will be under the theme of Parisian Glamour and Chic!

For the ones who join us from today, don’t worry, there are plenty of workshops, performance shows and parties left so you will feel that you’ve been here with us since one week already!!

SWEET "AU REVOIR"

@ HYATT REGENGY PARIS ETOILE

Are you ready for the last day of the Paris International Salsa Congress 2016? Hector Lavoe said “Todo tiene su final” but we in France we prefer to say “Ce n’est qu’un au revoir”, a sweet “au revoir”.. It’s only a Goodbye until we meet again very soon in .. 2017!

But let’s contain our tears drop for now, today is the day we spend all the energy that we have left. Why? Because it feels so good!! An endless day of workshops and exciting performance shows are waiting for you.

DRESS CODE: FEEL FREE

Tonight for the party, keep smiling, keep dancing under the intoxicating Latin rhythms, and express yourself freely with your own choice of outfit.
