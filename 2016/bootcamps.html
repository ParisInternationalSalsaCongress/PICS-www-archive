Bootcamps with the stars!!!

An intensive training program, created for all the dancers who want to challenge themselves.

Learn a choreography in 10 hours, share your passion with a motivated team and show your work on stage!! (Sunday night)

Bootcamps are created for all the dancers who want to challenge themselves and bring their dancing to the performance level.

For each bootcamp we accept max 30 participants. Be sure to book your bootcamp pass as soon as possible.
