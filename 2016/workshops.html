Workshops

Artists are a great source of inspiration to all of us salsa aficionados.

We love to see them perform and dance in their own style because they always provide us with surprise and excitement.

Of course the more we learn from these masters, the more skilled we become and.. the more fun we have on the dance floor!

We have selected for you a wide choice of workshops which will please you whatever  your style or your level! Salsa On1, On2, Cuban, Kizomba or Bachata.. Enjoy!

Please note that a few changes might appear in our workshop schedule
