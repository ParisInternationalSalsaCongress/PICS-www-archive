Bienvenue

We are very proud to invite you to the first edition of the Paris International Salsa Congress, held from April 19th to 24th 2016, at the very heart of Paris at the Hotel Hyatt Regency Etoile.

Get ready for an entire week of festivities, starting with pre-parties in the most famous dancing spots of the city, and then of course a very exhaustive non-stop program including: 50 workshops, 35 show performances, from 70 artists, 2 master boot camps, not forgetting social dance every night until dawn.

While enjoying the stunning beauty of the endless sites Paris can offer, you will have the privilege to dance with the best dancers coming from all over the world. All in a Parisian style Latin atmosphere! Join us and...
