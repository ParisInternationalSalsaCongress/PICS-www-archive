Mouaze & Ella

Paris

Mouaze Konaté is recognized in France and by his peers as a Master of New-York Salsa On2 and as one of the best dancers in Cha Cha Cha worldwide. Not only is he a very talented choreographer, but his performances always surprise the crowd with their originality and their uniqueness, thanks to the fine artistic and humoristic touch he puts on it.

He also devotes himself to helping his students to become stylish and playful dancers through his unique teaching method, which makes learning a fun experience; allowing you to progress faster than ever. Mouaze is an unconditional admirer of the Mambo King Eddie Torres, his endless source of inspiration not only for salsa but also spiritually.

Eleonora Jauk (Ella) from Croatia, but living now in Paris. She is the beautiful Salsa and life partner of Mouaze Konaté. Ella stands out with her technique, sensuality and her natural body movement. Before becoming a professional Salsa dancer she spent 7 years as a Ballroom dancer. She’s also an excellent teacher who is able to teach the ladies everything they need to take their dancing skills to the next level, both on the technical and feminine side.

Mouaze & Fanny

Paris

Fanny Frosini started Classic and Jazz dancing at a very early age at Studio Ellena in her native South of France.

In 2008 she discovered “Salsa”, fell in love with the dance and since that time the passion has never left her. Especially once she started taking Mouaze Konaté’s classes, she became a real addict and never stopped improving her technique.

Impressed by her determination to learn and her talent, it wasn’t long before Mouaze asked Fanny to become his dance partner. After a short training period with Mouaze, she was ready to perform and teach together with him at the most famous worldwide salsa congresses.

In 2011, Fanny created her first solo "Crucificar", which reflects perfectly a mix of her dancing background through Chachacha, Classic and Jazz. She definitely proved her great talent and ability as a choreographer.

Currently on her way to get the State diploma to become a Jazz Dance teacher, Fanny Frosini is a gifted dancer who is expressing her beautiful identity and multiple dance know-how through Salsa.

Jose Diaz & Euphoria Dance Company

Milano

Milan Italy is home to one of Salsa’s newest up and coming teams, Euphoria Dance Company.  Formed by Jose “La Salsa” Diaz in 2012 to bring a revived new feel to Mambo On2 in Italy. The company reflects the influence of New York Style On2 dancing with its creative interpretation and strong sense of musicality on stage.

Artistic Director Jose “La Salsa” Diaz teaches Mambo with an energetic Dominican Street Style of dancing. His incorporation of multiple turns, spins, turn combinations, counter turns, sudden breaks and changes of directions will leave his audience elated and wanting to see more. Euphoria Dance Company has created a syllabus which makes teaching and learning an easy and fun experience. “We consider ourselves not only instructors & performers but also ambassadors of Latin Music, Dance and Culture”.

Aude M.

Paris

The French glamour... Classical sternness coupled with latin fantasy... In brief Fire under the Ice!
Aude M started dancing at the early age of 4. She attended the famous Nancy's ballet conservatory and was awarded at the end of her academic classes at the age of 14.  She then learned various dances such as Jazz, but also character and folkloric dances.

Her ballroom dancing career started at age 20 with Rock N'Roll, then followed a few years later by Salsa, Chachacha and West Coast Swing.

She appeared on stage at several national and international scenes, especially since 2006 after she joined Mouaze Konaté's dance company. They will then performed together in many original shows: Nuestro Amor, Mission Impossible, AfroSpace, La Vie en Rose and PicaDulce. In 2011, she joined Big Spenders dance company where she performed 2 shows, Cubauza and Dos Gardenias.

Today, Aude M teaches her passion of dancing New York Style On2, Lady’s Bachata Moderna style and Latin Jazz. She has 2 of her own solo shows (Ella and Femme d’Ailleurs) and she also performs bachata in a duo show with Frantz Kichenassamy.

CUBAN

Alberto Valdes
& Andria Panagi

Italy

Alberto Valdes: One of the most famous cuban artist and instructors in the world, very often called "The Instructor of the Instructors", living in Milan, Italy for 20 years and owned one of the biggest and most known dance school "Cubabaila".

Dj-Viper "Willy"
